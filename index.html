About Me

ELITEMINT999 is a pixel artist and indie game designer who builds worlds from the ground up, crafting tilesets, characters, and UI systems that feel alive. Every asset starts with a question: does this serve the game? My process starts with gameplay feel, not aesthetics. I study how a scene needs to read in motion, what the player's eye should chase, and where atmosphere should breathe. From there I build in layers - silhouette first, color story second, detail last. The result is pixel art that holds up at any scale and pulls players deeper into the world.
